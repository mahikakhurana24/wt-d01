Bubbles

Powerpuff Girl

Summary

Bubbles is one of the three main protagonists of the animated television series The Powerpuff Girls. She fights crime with her sisters Blossom and Buttercup. She is the Joy and the Laughter; she is the emotional glue of the superhero trio. She is the "sugar" ingredient of the three.

Appearence and Personality

Bubbles is portrayed as having blonde hair in two pigtails and a neat, curved fashion and sky blue eyes. Her dresses are sky blue with a black-centered stripe and she wears white tights and black Mary Janes. The outro refers to her as "the joy and laughter," meaning that she is defined as very sweet in personality.
